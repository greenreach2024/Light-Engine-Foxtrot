## Step 1: Create Your Buyer Account

Click "Register as Buyer" or "Sign Up"

### 1.2 Complete Buyer Profile

**Required Information:**

- **Business Type**: Restaurant, Café, Caterer, Retail Store, Individual Consumer

Configure your default settings:

- **Delivery preference**: Farm pickup vs. delivery (if available)

## Step 4: Payment Setup

### 4.1 Payment Methods (Pilot Program)

**Method 1: Manual Payment (Default for Pilot)**
- Order is confirmed but payment processed manually
- Invoice sent via email
- Pay via e-transfer, check, or cash on pickup
- Payment due within 7 days

**Method 2: Credit Card** (if enrolled)
- Card charged when farm confirms order
- Secure payment via Stripe
- 3.5% processing fee included in platform fee
- Immediate confirmation

**Method 3: Invoice Terms** (approved accounts only)
- Net 30 payment terms for established businesses
- Requires credit application and approval
- Minimum order history required

### 4.2 Payment Security

All payments are secured by:
- **Escrow System**: Payment held until delivery confirmed
- **Buyer Protection**: Refund if order not fulfilled
- **Farm Protection**: Payment guaranteed after delivery
- **Encrypted Transactions**: PCI-compliant payment processing

### 4.3 Set Default Payment Method

In your account settings:
1. Go to "Payment Methods"
2. Add credit card or select invoice terms
3. Set as default for faster checkout
4. Update billing address if needed

## Step 5: Order Tracking & Communication

### 5.1 Order Status Flow

After placing an order, track status:

1. **PENDING VERIFICATION** (0-24 hours)
   - Farm reviewing your order
   - Checking inventory availability
   - May propose modifications

2. **VERIFIED** (farm confirmed)
   - Order confirmed by farm
   - Fulfillment in progress
   - Pickup/delivery scheduled

3. **READY FOR PICKUP** / **OUT FOR DELIVERY**
   - Order packed and ready
   - Pickup window available or delivery en route
   - You'll receive notification

4. **COMPLETED**
   - Order delivered/picked up
   - Confirm receipt in portal
   - Payment released to farm

**Possible Alternative Statuses:**
- **MODIFIED**: Farm proposed quantity adjustments
- **SEEKING ALTERNATIVES**: Original farm unavailable
- **CANCELLED**: Order cancelled (full refund)

### 5.2 Notifications You'll Receive

**Order Placed:**
- ✅ "Order #12345 submitted - awaiting farm verification"
- Sent immediately via email

**Farm Response:**
- ✅ "Order #12345 confirmed by Green Valley Farm"
- ✅ "Order #12345 modified - review changes"
- ⚠️ "Order #12345 declined - seeking alternative farms"

**Fulfillment Updates:**
- 📦 "Order #12345 ready for pickup - pickup by tomorrow 5pm"
- 🚚 "Order #12345 out for delivery - arrives 2pm-4pm today"

**Deadline Warnings:**
- ⏰ "Farm verification deadline expiring - order may be reassigned"
- ⏰ "Pickup deadline approaching - pickup by 5pm or order cancelled"

### 5.3 Communicate with Farms

**Contact Farm Directly:**
- Click "Message Farm" in order details
- Email sent to farm contact
- Farm can respond via email or phone

**Common reasons to contact farm:**
- Modify order before verification
- Request specific varieties/sizes
- Coordinate special pickup arrangements
- Ask about product details
- Provide delivery access instructions

## Step 6: Receiving Your Order

### 6.1 Pickup Instructions

**Before pickup:**
- Check notification for exact pickup window
- Note any special instructions from farm
- Bring vehicle suitable for load
- Have order number ready (#12345)

**At pickup:**
- Arrive during specified window
- Identify yourself with order number
- Inspect products (check quality and quantities)
- Sign packing slip to confirm receipt
- Report any issues immediately

### 6.2 Delivery Instructions

**Before delivery:**
- Be available during delivery window
- Ensure delivery location is accessible
- Have cooler space ready for perishables
- Check SMS/email for driver updates

**At delivery:**
- Inspect products upon arrival
- Check quantities against packing slip
- Report any issues to driver and via portal
- Sign to confirm receipt

### 6.3 Quality Issues

If there's a problem with your order:

**Minor Issues** (cosmetic damage, slight quantity shortage):
- Document with photos
- Report via portal within 24 hours
- Partial refund or credit issued

**Major Issues** (spoiled product, wrong items, large shortage):
- Contact farm AND ops@greenreachgreens.com immediately
- Do not accept delivery if severely incorrect
- Full refund processed within 48 hours

**Dispute Resolution:**
- Most issues resolved directly with farm
- GreenReach mediates if needed
- All communications logged for transparency

## Step 7: Confirm Delivery & Payment Release

### 7.1 Confirm Receipt

**CRITICAL STEP:**

After receiving your order, you must confirm delivery:
1. Log in to wholesale portal
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Find the order
4. Click "Confirm Delivery"

**Why this matters:**
- Payment is held in escrow until you confirm
- Confirming releases payment to the farm
- Farms depend on timely payment release
- If you don't confirm within 48 hours, payment auto-releases

### 7.2 Rate Your Experience

After confirming delivery, please rate:
- **Product Quality** (5 stars)
- **Farm Communication** (5 stars)
- **Delivery/Pickup Experience** (5 stars)
- **Overall Satisfaction** (5 stars)

Add comments:
- What went well?
- What could be improved?
- Would you order from this farm again?

**Your feedback helps:**
- Other buyers make informed decisions
- Farms improve their service
- GreenReach optimize the platform

## 💰 Pricing & Fees

### Understanding Costs

**What you pay:**
- **Product prices**: Set by each farm (wholesale rates)
- **Delivery fee**: Varies by distance (if delivery selected)
- **Platform fee**: 3% of order value (covers payment processing, escrow, technology)

**What you DON'T pay:**
- Membership fees (none during pilot)
- Listing fees
- Hidden charges

**Example breakdown:**
```
Products: $200.00
Delivery: $10.00
Platform fee (3% of products): $6.00
TOTAL: $216.00
```

### Discounts & Promotions

**Pilot program benefits:**
- No membership fee (normally $25/month)
- First order: Free delivery (up to $15)
- Refer a buyer: $10 credit
- Refer a farm: $25 credit

**Volume discounts:**
- Some farms offer bulk pricing
- Discounts shown automatically in cart
- Negotiate custom pricing for large recurring orders

## 🆘 Troubleshooting & Support

### Common Issues

**Problem: Can't find a specific product**
- Use search bar with specific keywords
- Try broader category filters
- Some products seasonal - check back later
- Request product from ops@greenreachgreens.com

**Problem: Order not verified by farm**
- Farms have 24 hours to respond
- Check order status for updates
- If deadline passes, alternative farms automatically contacted
- You'll receive notification of any changes

**Problem: Need to cancel an order**
- Before farm verification: Cancel anytime, full refund
- After farm verification: Contact farm directly
- Within 24 hours of pickup/delivery: Cancellation fee may apply
- Use "Cancel Order" button in order details

**Problem: Payment not processing**
- Check credit card details are correct
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact your bank if card declined

## 🌟 Buyer Best Practices

### 1. Plan Ahead
- Order at least 48 hours in advance
- Check farm lead times
- Consider harvest schedules (products fresher mid-week)

### 2. Communicate Clearly
- Provide detailed delivery instructions
- Respond promptly to farm messages
- Report issues immediately, not days later

### 3. Be Flexible
- Farms may propose substitutions
- Quantities may vary slightly (natural products)
- Weather affects harvests - be understanding

### 4. Confirm Deliveries Promptly
- Confirm within 24 hours
- Farms need payment release
- Helps maintain good relationships

### 5. Leave Feedback
- Rate every order
- Help other buyers and farms
- Constructive feedback improves platform

### 6. Build Relationships
- Favor farms with good service
- Communicate your needs
- Regular orders help farms plan
- You'll get better service as a repeat customer

Growing is the Easy Part

With complete seed-to-sale visibility, you stay in control from planning to payment. Track and forecast inventory, spot demand trends, and use AI-assisted price checks to validate what you charge. Built-in point-of-sale plus online and wholesale channels help ensure what you grow gets sold—on time, at the right price, with less waste.

Inventory Management

Inventory that updates itself. Forecast what's coming next with planting and harvest schedules, and track performance with farm and regional crop trends. Know what you have, what's next, and when it's ready to sell.

Point of Sale

Accept payments, print receipts, and track daily sales—fully linked to live inventory. Every sale updates stock automatically and creates a traceable record from harvest to customer, supporting audit and reporting requirements.

Online Store

Launch your farm's online store in minutes—connected to live inventory. Use a custom subdomain, publish your product catalog, accept checkout orders, and manage fulfillment from harvest to pickup or delivery.

Wholesale Marketplace

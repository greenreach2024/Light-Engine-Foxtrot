Why This Matters

Canada imports billions in produce that could be grown locally year-round. Here's what changes when communities show demand.

🌱

Investment Follows Demand

Local vertical farms need to see community interest before they invest. Your signup is a signal that your area is ready for year-round local produce.

🏡

Fresher Food, Less Travel

Field produce from California or Mexico travels 5,000+ km over 10–21 days. Local vertical farm produce travels under 50 km and arrives within 24 hours of harvest.

❄️

Four-Season Agriculture

Canada's growing season is short, but indoor farming changes that. Year-round production means year-round local jobs and food security in every community.

🤝

Strengthen Your Community

Every dollar spent on local food circulates 3x more in your community. Your signup tells growers the demand is real — and worth investing in.

📊

Data-Driven Decisions

We connect this demand data with local growers, investors, and agricultural planners to direct resources where they'll have the greatest impact.

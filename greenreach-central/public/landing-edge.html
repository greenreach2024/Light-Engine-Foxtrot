The ultimate urban growing solution—built to make indoor farming easier, more efficient, and community-driven. Streamlined workflows, reduced labour, and crop diversity you won't find anywhere else. No green thumb needed.

Capabilities

Professional grow results—no experience required.

Built for community organizations, volunteer teams, and first-time farm operators. Our system manages climate, lighting, and nutrients automatically using proven targets and crop recipes—monitoring performance, making micro-adjustments, and adapting to your unique space over time.

Automated Lighting

Optimized lighting, automatically. We adjust light levels as crops grow to deliver what plants need—reducing energy waste and plant stress for more consistent harvests and a more efficient farm.

Climate Control

A grow room that learns your space. Smart circulation fans help create distinct zones within a single room, so different crops can thrive side-by-side. We automatically manage temperature and humidity, reusing heat and moisture where possible—cutting waste while keeping plants comfortable and consistent.

Nutrient Management Integration

Multi-crop nutrients made simple. Choose what you're growing, and the system automatically applies the right nutrient recipe—matched to the specific seed and growth stage. No guessing, no research, no manual mixing decisions. From seed to harvest, consistent results every time.

Early Issue Detection

Artificial intelligence–assisted monitoring watches your equipment and growing conditions around the clock. It flags unusual changes early—like airflow dropping, humidity drifting, or a crop showing signs of stress—so you can act before quality or yield is affected. Your built-in assistant explains what changed and suggests what to check next.

Seed to Sale

Know what to seed, when to harvest, and what you'll have available—without spreadsheets. Inventory is captured automatically and stays connected from the grow room to retail and wholesale ordering. As crops are seeded and harvested, current and future availability updates in real time, so buyers can order with confidence and you can fulfill on time. Built-in traceability keeps every batch documented and ready for compliance.

Cloud Sync

Optional cloud synchronization for remote monitoring, multi-site management, and team collaboration across locations.

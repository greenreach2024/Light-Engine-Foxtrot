How GreenReach Works

From farm to your door in four simple steps

1

Browse Unified Catalog

View products from all participating farms in one unified catalog. Filter by certifications, sustainable practices, and distance from your location.

2

Place Order with Payment Hold

Add items to cart from multiple farms. Enter delivery details and payment method. We authorize (not charge) your card to reserve funds while farms verify availability.

3

Farms Verify & Commit

Your order is automatically split by farm. Each farm has 24 hours to verify they can fulfill their portion. If a farm declines, we automatically source from alternatives.

4

Harvest, Pack & Deliver

Once all farms verify, payment is captured and farms harvest your items. Orders are packed with FDA/CFIA-compliant lot codes and delivered via approved cold-chain logistics.

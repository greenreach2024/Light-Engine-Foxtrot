Program Status

This page reflects current operational status, not future marketing promises.

WHS

Wholesale-Only Delivery

Delivery is currently tied to the wholesale ordering workflow. Access is intended for approved business and institutional accounts.

ZONE

Farm-Configured Coverage

Coverage is controlled by farm delivery settings and zone rules in the platform. Availability varies by farm and active service area.

ROLL

Staged Rollout

Regions are enabled progressively. Some farms may be active while others are still in setup or onboarding.

OPS

Operational Dispatch

Delivery operations are dispatched through farm/admin workflows, with route and status coordination handled in internal tools.

LEDG

Financial Recordkeeping

Delivery fees, driver payouts, and platform margin are now recorded and available in reconciliation and CSV export workflows.

INFO

Not Yet General Availability

This is not a universal consumer delivery program today. If your geography is not live, create a profile to receive updates.

How It Works

Current delivery flow for wholesale operations.

1

Create Wholesale Profile

Sign up in the wholesale portal and complete account details for your organization.

2

Farm Delivery Rules Apply

Eligibility and fee quotes are determined by farm delivery settings, zones, and minimum order requirements.

3

Order + Fee Ledger Created

When delivery checkout executes, delivery fee, payout, and margin records are written for operational and finance workflows.

4

Dispatch and Reconciliation

Operations manage driver assignment and payout status, and finance teams can reconcile/export delivery records by farm and day.

Where We Are Today

Delivery is growing. Here's what's available now and what's still in progress.

Available Now

  • Wholesale delivery in areas where farms have opted in
  • Each farm sets its own delivery zones and minimum order sizes
  • Delivery fees and driver payments are tracked per order
  • Farms can review their delivery records and export reports
  • Drivers can apply and get started through our enrollment process

Coming Soon

  • Home delivery in all areas (currently limited to participating zones)
  • Same-day delivery (timing depends on farm and driver availability)
  • Standardized delivery pricing (each farm currently sets its own fees)
  • Recurring subscription deliveries for individual buyers
  • Full coverage across all regions (we're expanding as farms join)

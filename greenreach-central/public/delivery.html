How It Works

Getting fresh, local produce delivered is simple.

1

Browse & Order

Explore available produce from local farms through our wholesale portal. Select what you need and choose a delivery window.

2

Morning Harvest

Your order triggers a harvest at the farm. Produce is picked fresh the morning of your scheduled delivery day.

3

Quality Check

Every item is inspected and carefully packed to maintain freshness, quality, and food safety standards during transit.

4

Delivered to You

A local GreenReach driver delivers your order to your door. Track the delivery in real time from farm to table.

      Do you have a Square account?

      Square is a secure payment processor that allows you to accept credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments from your customers.

      Why Square?

      • Industry-leading security and PCI compliance
      • Competitive processing rates (2.6% + 10¢ per transaction)
      • Fast deposits (1-2 business days)
      • Built-in fraud protection
      • 24/7 customer support
      Important: This is YOUR Square account for your farm business. GreenReach only processes subscription fees for the Light Engine service.

      Connect Your Square Account

      Click the button below to securely connect your Square account to your farm store. You'll be redirected to Square to authorize the connection.

      What permissions are requested?

      • Read Business Profile: Display your business name and location
      • Manage Orders: Track and fulfill customer orders

Add email alert notifications to nightly audit service

- Import emailService into nightly-audit.js
- New sendAuditAlert() sends HTML + text email on failures or warnings
- Email includes: failure/warning callout boxes, full check table,
  status icons, actionable detail for each failed check
- Recipient configured via ADMIN_ALERT_EMAIL or ADMIN_EMAIL env var
- Graceful skip when no alert email is configured
- Non-blocking: alert email errors are logged but do not fail the audit

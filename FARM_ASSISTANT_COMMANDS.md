# 🤖 Farm Assistant - Command Reference

**Perfect for Hospital Education, Children & Non-Skilled Users**

The AI Farm Assistant is designed to be **super easy** - just say what you want naturally! 
No need for perfect grammar or exact phrases - it understands!

---

## 💡 How It Works

The assistant uses **simple keyword detection** - it looks for key words in whatever you type:

- **"today's harvest"** ✅ (has "harvest")
- **"harvest today"** ✅ (has "harvest")  
- **"ready"** ✅ (harvest-related word)
- **"pick"** ✅ (harvest-related word)
- **"temp"** ✅ (shows temperature)
- **"basil"** ✅ (searches for basil)

Just type naturally - it will understand! 🎯

---

## 🌱 Harvest Commands

Just mention **harvest** or **ready** in your question:

- **"harvest"** - Shows what's ready
- **"today's harvest"** ✅
- **"harvest today"** ✅  
- **"ready"** - Shows ready crops
- **"pick"** - Same as harvest
- **"what's ready"** ✅

**Any variation works!** Just include harvest/ready/pick/ripe in your question.

## 🌡️ Environment Commands

Just mention **temp** or **humidity**:

- **"temp"** - Shows temperature
- **"temperature"** ✅
- **"hot"** - Shows temp ✅
- **"humidity"** - Shows humidity ✅
- **"humid"** ✅
- **"conditions"** - Shows both ✅

**Super simple!** Just say temp, hot, cold, humidity, etc.

## 🥬 Find Crops

Just say the crop name OR ask about it:

- **"basil"** ✅
- **"lettuce"** ✅
- **"where basil"** ✅
- **"find lettuce"** ✅
- **"show me tomatoes"** ✅
- **"do we have kale"** ✅

**Works with any phrasing!** The assistant knows these crops:
- lettuce, basil, tomato, kale, spinach, arugula, chard
- herbs, romaine, microgreens, salad greens

---

## 💡 Hardware Control

Say **blink** + crop or zone name:

- **"blink basil"** ✅
- **"blink zone A"** ✅
- **"light basil"** ✅
- **"flash lights"** ✅

Lights will blink for 10 seconds to show you where it is!

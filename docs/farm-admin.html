AI Pricing Assistant

The AI Pricing Assistant analyzes North American organic produce markets to recommend competitive pricing for your crops. It searches retail pricing data from both US and Canadian retailers (Sobeys, Metro, Loblaws, Farm Boy, Whole Foods, etc.), monitors market trends, and alerts you to significant price changes. Prices from Canadian retailers are shown in CAD with no currency conversion.

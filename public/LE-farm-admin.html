💡 Export Tips

📦 Inventory Export

Use for monthly inventory reconciliation and valuation. Import into accounting software to match physical counts with system records.

💰 Sales Export

Use summary level for daily/weekly reports. Use detail level for tax filing and detailed product analysis. Date range required.

📊 QuickBooks

Import daily to QuickBooks Online via CSV import. Automatically maps to Chart of Accounts. Export one day at a time for best results.

AI Pricing Assistant

The AI Pricing Assistant analyzes North American organic produce markets to recommend competitive pricing for your crops. It searches retail pricing data from both US and Canadian retailers (Sobeys, Metro, Loblaws, Farm Boy, Whole Foods, etc.), monitors market trends, and alerts you to significant price changes. Prices from Canadian retailers are shown in CAD with no currency conversion.

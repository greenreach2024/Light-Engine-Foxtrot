Research Workspace

Run studies, manage datasets, maintain electronic lab notebooks, and coordinate collaborators -- all within your Light Engine farm environment. Research features are available as an add-on tier for qualifying farms.

Study Design

Create and manage research studies with structured protocols, treatment groups, milestones, and deviation tracking.

  • Protocol versioning and review
  • Treatment group management
  • Milestone and timeline tracking

Data Management

Structured datasets with full provenance, calibration logs, and observation recording tied to your farm environment.

  • Observation recording with provenance
  • Device calibration tracking
  • Export to JSON, CSV, or Excel

Electronic Lab Notebook

Document experiments, attach files, and maintain a signed audit trail for compliance and reproducibility.

  • Notebook entries with attachments
  • Digital signatures and snapshots
  • Reusable entry templates

Compliance

Data management plans, retention policies, budget tracking, and citation management for grant-funded or regulated work.

  • Data management plans (DMPs)
  • Budget line items and tracking
  • Citation and closeout records

Collaboration

Invite collaborators with role-based access, share study data, and coordinate reviews across your research team.

  • Role-based collaborator access
  • Review comments and feedback
  • Secure share links

AI-Assisted Research

Research-tier farms have access to AI tools that integrate with study data, calibration status, and observation history.

  • Study timeline and budget queries
  • Observation and dataset summaries
  • Calibration status checks

Research workspace features require an active research-tier subscription. Contact your account administrator for access.

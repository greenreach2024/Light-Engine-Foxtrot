Capabilities

Built for Professional Growers

Enterprise-grade automation with hardware integration, ML anomaly detection, and touchscreen control for commercial growing operations.

Automated Lighting

DMX512 control for professional grow lights. Sunrise/sunset simulation, DLI optimization, and per-spectrum intensity control.

Climate Control

Precision temperature, humidity, and VPD management. Automated HVAC, dehumidifiers, and CO₂ injection based on growth stage.

Sensor Integration

Atlas Scientific pH/EC sensors, PAR meters, soil moisture probes. Modbus RTU and RS-485 protocols supported.

IsolationForest algorithms detect equipment failures, environmental anomalies, and pest/disease outbreaks before they impact yield.

Optional cloud synchronization for remote monitoring, multi-site management, and team collaboration across locations.

Quick Start

Your Symcod W101M device is ready to use - just power on and follow the touchscreen wizard.

For Growers
Pre-Installed & Ready

Your Symcod W101M ships with Light Engine already installed. No downloads, no installation, no setup commands. Just power on.

Getting Started with Symcod W101M

Step 1: Physical Setup

  1. Unpack your Symcod W101M device
  2. Connect power adapter (included)
  3. Connect ethernet cable to your router (for initial setup)
  4. Press power button
  5. Device boots in 30 seconds - touchscreen lights up

Step 2: Touchscreen Setup Wizard

The setup wizard guides you through 6 simple steps. Takes about 10 minutes:

1
Welcome
Tap "Start Setup"
2
Network
Connect to WiFi (or use ethernet)
3
Farm Info
Enter farm name and location
4
Certifications
Select certifications (USDA Organic, GlobalGAP, etc.)
5
Hardware
System scans for sensors automatically
6
Complete
Dashboard launches automatically
What You Need

Before starting setup, have ready:

  1. WiFi password (if not using ethernet)
  2. Farm name (example: "Oakwood Farms")
  3. Certification info (if your farm has certifications)

Accessing Your Dashboard

After setup completes, access your dashboard three ways:

On the Symcod Touchscreen

Dashboard opens automatically on the device screen. Use it like an iPad.

From Your Computer or Tablet

Open any browser and go to:

  • http://light-engine.local (works on most networks)
  • Or use the IP address shown on Symcod screen

Via Cloud Portal

Access your farm from anywhere at portal.greenreach.io

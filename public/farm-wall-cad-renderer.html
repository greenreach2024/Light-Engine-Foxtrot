📋 Instructions

  1. Enter your wall dimensions in inches
  2. Adjust scale for desired output size
  3. Click "Generate Drawing" to create the layout
  4. Toggle layers on/off to view different combinations
  5. Click "Save as PDF" and use your browser's print dialog (Cmd/Ctrl+P)
  6. Choose "Save as PDF" in the print destination

Tip: Generate two PDFs - one with just plumbing/electrical, and another with grow racks visible for installation reference.

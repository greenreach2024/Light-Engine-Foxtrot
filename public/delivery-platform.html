Buyer & Receiver Roles

Right access, right people

Wholesale accounts support two roles. The person ordering and managing billing shouldn't be blocked by dock operations—and the receiver at the dock shouldn't see payment details.

👤
Buyer Admin
Full account control
  • Browse catalog & place orders
  • Manage invoices, terms, credits
  • Invite team members & assign roles
  • View billing statements & payment history
  • Set delivery preferences & standing orders
📦
Receiver
Dock & delivery operations
  • Track deliveries in real time
  • View POD — signature, photos, pin
  • Report issues — damage, partial, missing
  • Set delivery window preferences
  • No access to billing, invoices, or pricing
Receiver Invite Flow
Buyer Admin → "Team" tab → Invite → Email sent → Set password → role = RECEIVER

Automated Driver Payments

No POD, no payout

A stop becomes payable only when it has a timestamp, a POD artifact, GPS coordinates, and an exception code (if applicable). Once locked, pay statements are immutable—adjustments become separate lines.

  • POD gate: delivery timestamp + artifact + GPS required
  • Stripe Connect for contractor payouts
  • Manual payouts hold funds until delivery confirmed
  • Weekly pay period (Monday–Sunday), pay day Friday
  • Minimum guarantee: route pay ≥ min rate × engaged minutes
  • DPWRA compliant: pay period, pay day, assignment details
🇨🇦 Ontario DPWRA (July 1, 2025)
Once a driver becomes ACTIVE, the platform provides pay calculation details, pay periods, pay day, and assignment start/finish times. "Engaged time" = accept to finish.
Payout Rules Engine

Configurable settlement policy

Pay formula, minimum guarantees, exception handling, and hold/release rules—all defined in a single policy config that the settlement engine evaluates automatically.

payout_policy: pay_period: frequency: WEEKLY period_start: MONDAY pay_day: FRIDAY timezone: America/Toronto payable_gate: require_pod: true pod_types_allowed: [SIG, PHOTO, PIN] hold_if_exception_open: true driver_pay_formula: base: 15.00 per_km: 0.55 per_engaged_min: 0.18 per_stop: 1.25 wait: grace_min: 10 per_wait_min: 0.20 minimum_guarantee: enabled: true rule: "pay ≥ min_rate × engaged_min"

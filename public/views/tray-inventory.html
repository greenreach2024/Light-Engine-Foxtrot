Install Activity Hub on iPad

  1. Tap the Share button ⎋ in Safari's toolbar
    It's at the top-right corner (or bottom on iPhone)
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" 📱
    You may need to scroll down in the share menu
  3. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
  4. Find the Activity Hub icon on your iPad home screen 🎉
✨ Pro Tip
Once installed, the Activity Hub will work offline and load instantly - perfect for the farm floor!
